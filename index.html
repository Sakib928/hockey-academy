Our Equipment

Welcome to our state-of-the-art hockey academy, where excellence meets innovation in the realm of player development. Our commitment to fostering the next generation of hockey stars is reflected in the cutting-edge equipment and facilities we provide to our athletes.

Hockey Training

Our coaching staff is committed to honing the unique skills of each player. Through personalized training sessions, athletes receive targeted instruction to enhance their skating, shooting, puck-handling, and defensive abilities. Our focus on individualized skill development allows players to unlock their full potential and elevate their game to new heights.

Private Lessons

Our coaching staff maintains an ongoing dialogue with players, providing constructive feedback and setting clear goals for improvement. Regular evaluations help players track their progress, identify areas for growth, and adjust their training focus accordingly. This iterative feedback loop is essential for the continuous development of each athlete.

Ski Touring

Ski touring is an excellent cross-training activity that complements the physical demands of hockey. It engages various muscle groups, particularly the lower body, helping to build strength, endurance, and cardiovascular fitness. The dynamic nature of skiing on varied terrains contributes to improved balance and agility, essential elements for any elite athlete.

Booking

Our hockey academy offers a convenient online reservation portal, allowing prospective athletes, teams, and coaches to browse available time slots, facilities, and programs. This user-friendly platform provides a transparent overview of our offerings, making it simple for users to select the options that best align with their training needs.

Pricings

Our hockey academy offers flexible membership plans designed to accommodate various training frequencies and durations. Whether you're a committed individual player, a dedicated team, or a coach looking for consistent access to our facilities, our membership plans provide options that align with your specific training goals.
